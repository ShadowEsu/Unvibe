# Legal review checklist

- [ ] Company/operator identity, addresses, contacts, governing law, age limit, and effective dates finalized.
- [ ] Product data flow and inventory compared against the deployed build, hosting settings, provider, waitlist, and support tools.
- [ ] Privacy notice covers code/context, learning metadata, auth, skills, consent history, logs, provider/subprocessors, transfers, rights, security, and contact.
- [ ] Terms cover beta availability, AI limitations, code ownership/responsibility, acceptable use, service changes, termination, warranties/liability, and dispute terms.
- [ ] Retention periods/backups/purge jobs and deletion-on-restore process approved and implemented.
- [ ] Account deletion covers custom database, external Auth identity, local data, backups/logs, and verification timeline accurately.
- [ ] AI provider training/retention/security statements verified for the exact contract/account; no absolute privacy/security claim remains.
- [ ] “Free beta” wording states no current charge/credit card, avoids permanence/unlimited-service promises, and defines notice/consent before future charging.
- [ ] Billing/subscription/cancellation text is hidden or clearly inapplicable while billing is disabled.
- [ ] Marketing, app UI, metadata, onboarding, support macros, and legal pages use the same approved wording.
- [ ] Accessibility, export/access requests, security reporting, jurisdictional rights, and minors handling reviewed.
- [ ] Approval evidence includes counsel name, date, document commit/hash, published URLs, and required re-review triggers.

External beta is blocked until qualified counsel approves the final published text. Engineering-authored drafts are not approval.
