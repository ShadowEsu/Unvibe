# Ownership & IP

**Status: private and proprietary.** This repository and product ("Unvibe", codename; product
name "Uncode") are not open source.

## Ownership
- All original source code, designs, copy, illustrations, the logo, and the visual system in this
  repository are owned by the founder (or the founder's company, once incorporated). "Founder"
  means Preston Jay Susanto until assigned to a company entity.
- No third-party code, trademark, or asset is claimed as the founder's own. Third-party libraries
  remain under their original licenses (see `docs/licenses.md`).
- The design is *inspired by* the interaction quality of tools like Wispr Flow but uses original
  assets, wording, fonts, and icons. No proprietary third-party assets are copied.

## Contributors
- Any outside contributor (employee, contractor, freelancer, or collaborator) must sign an
  **IP-assignment and confidentiality agreement** before contributing, assigning all rights in
  their contributions to the founder/company.
- Contributions without a signed agreement must not be merged.

## Third-party dependencies
- Third-party libraries retain their original licenses. The project's use of them does not
  transfer their ownership to the founder.
- See `docs/licenses.md` for the dependency license report. No copyleft licenses are present.

## Distribution
- This project **may not be open-sourced, published, or redistributed** without the founder's
  written approval.
- Internal builds, TestFlight/beta distributions, and App Store submissions are permitted under
  the founder's direction.

_This document states intent and is not a substitute for executed legal agreements. Have counsel
prepare the actual IP-assignment, contributor, and employment agreements before onboarding anyone._
